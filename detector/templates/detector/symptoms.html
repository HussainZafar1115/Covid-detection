COVID-19 Symptoms & Care Guidelines

Important Notice

If you're experiencing severe symptoms, immediately call the Pakistan COVID-19 Helpline at 1166 or visit your nearest COVID-19 facility.

Common Symptoms

  • Fever or chills
  • Dry cough
  • Fatigue
  • Difficulty breathing
  • Loss of taste or smell
  • Body aches

What to Do

  1. Isolate yourself immediately
  2. Wear a mask around others
  3. Contact your local healthcare provider
  4. Monitor your oxygen levels
  5. Stay hydrated and rest
  6. Take paracetamol for fever

Emergency Warning Signs

Seek immediate medical attention if you experience:

  • Difficulty breathing or shortness of breath
  • Persistent chest pain or pressure
  • Confusion or inability to stay awake
  • Bluish lips or face
