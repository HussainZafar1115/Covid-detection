COVID-19 Prevention Tips

Wear a Mask

Wear a properly fitted mask when in public or around others, especially in indoor settings.

Social Distance

Maintain at least 6 feet (2 meters) distance from others, especially if you're indoors.

Hand Hygiene

Wash your hands frequently with soap and water for at least 20 seconds.

Vaccination

Get vaccinated and stay up to date with recommended boosters.

Ventilation

Ensure good ventilation in indoor spaces by opening windows or using air purifiers.

Monitor Health

Watch for symptoms and get tested if you experience any COVID-19 symptoms.
